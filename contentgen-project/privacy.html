Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

ContentGen Pro ("the app") is a personal content-creation tool. This page explains what happens when you connect a social media account.

What we access

When you connect a social media account (such as LinkedIn), the app requests only the minimum permissions needed to publish posts on your behalf and to identify your account. We do not read your existing posts, messages, contacts, or any other personal data beyond your basic profile information (name and account identifier).

What we store

We store your access token securely on our server so that you don't have to log in every time you want to post. We do not sell, share, or use this data for advertising. We do not store the content of posts beyond what's needed to publish them.

How to disconnect

You can disconnect your account at any time from within the app, or by revoking access directly from your social media platform's own account settings (e.g. LinkedIn's "Permitted Services" page).

Third parties

We do not share your data with any third party. Posting requests go directly from our server to the social media platform's own official API.

Contact

This is a personal project. For questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact the app owner directly.
